// 01 — ABOUT

From site audits to security audits.

The frameworks changed. The discipline didn't.

For over 15 years I led quality assurance, risk management, and project delivery on some of the region's most demanding builds — Microsoft's SIN06 data center, Siltronic's semiconductor FAB, and multi-country O&G programmes across the UAE, Oman, and Kuwait.

Every one of those roles came down to the same core skill: finding risk before it finds you — through inspections, NCR tracking, root cause analysis, and compliance documentation. In 2026 I formalised that instinct for cybersecurity, earning my Google Cybersecurity Certificate and building a home lab to prove it hands-on.

Today I document real risk registers, incident response reports, and vulnerability assessments — mapped to NIST CSF 2.0, ISO 27001, and MITRE ATT&CK — the same rigor I once brought to ISO-compliant QMS on mission-critical builds.
